    What to do

    Clicking a color key item saves it to "history" and sets it as the first color in "standard combinations" (the "MD" bars show closest Material-Design matches). The top nav and the footer are set to "Complement MD." The picker background is set to"Complement ryb MD" from the Adobe alternate color wheel. Watch the top pattern and tooltips too.

    Check Boxes

    "Lock" doesn't allow non-Material-Color.

    "Limit" further restricts to "500" Material Colors

    Question: How do Google's Material Design colors relate to "standard" color-theory combinations like Comlement and Triad? Pay around and have fun.

    What to do

    Setting the "Pick a Color" or clicking any of the color key bars sets it as the main first color in "standard combinations" (the "MD" bars display closest Material-Design palette matches) and puts that pick in "history." The top nav and the footer are set to the two colors of the new "Complement MD."
